What Is It

What Is Paper Trading?

Paper trading is simulated trading where you place virtual buy and sell orders using real market prices — but without any real money at risk. The name comes from the old practice of writing down hypothetical trades on paper to track them.

It is the safest way to test a new strategy, practice options execution, or build trading confidence before committing real capital. Every experienced trader uses paper trading at some stage.

TradeDiary's paper trading uses live NSE market data, so your simulation reflects real prices, spreads, and market conditions.

Who Should Paper Trade?

  • ✓ Experienced traders testing a new strategy
